\section{Professional Misadventures}

\subsection{Cloud Architect at LBBW (Nov 2024 -- Present)}
\textbf{Mission:} Transform Germany's major state bank IT to cloud\\
\textbf{Reality:} Discovered "Skywalker" platform unused in production after year of development

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item \fail{Platform Gap Champion} -- Identified so many missing features in Skywalker that I became the unofficial "Platform Enhancement Request Generator"
\item \fail{Multi-Cloud Dreamer} -- Proposed Elastisys Welkin integration; committee still discussing vendor lock-in fears while AWS laughs
\item \fail{Meeting Marathon Runner} -- Achieved personal record: 8 stakeholder meetings in one day, zero decisions made

\subsection{Cloud Architect at Union Investment (Oct 2021 -- Dec 2024)}

\textbf{Reality:} 3+ year journey through the valley of distributed systems

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item \fail{Monolith Slayer} -- Decomposed one monolith into 8 microservices; now have 8 problems instead of 1
\item \fail{Event Stream Philosopher} -- Designed event-driven architecture; spent months debating what constitutes an "event"
\item \fail{Microfrontend Evangelist} -- Convinced everyone microfrontends were the future; debugging cross-domain issues became new full-time job
