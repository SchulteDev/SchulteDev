\section{Professional Failures \& Rejections}

\textbf{Reality Check:} Discovered the "production-ready" Skywalker platform had never seen production. Currently cataloging applications older than most cloud technologies. \\
\textbf{Lesson:} "Phoenix" projects often burn architects first.

\subsection{Cloud Architect at Union Investment (Oct 2021 -- Dec 2024)}
\textbf{The Monolith Massacre:} Spent 3 years decomposing one monolith into 8 microservices. Original estimate: 6 months. \\
\textbf{Over-Engineering Award:} Built a microfrontend framework so complex, needed workshops to explain it to my own team. \\
\textbf{Testing Obsession:} Wrote 1000+ tests achieving 10-minute builds. Time saved: 20 minutes/day. Time spent optimizing: 3 months. \\
\textbf{Lesson:} Sometimes "micro" means microscopic progress.

\subsection{Middleware Engineer at Saloodo!/DHL (Dec 2021 -- Nov 2024)}
\textbf{Solo Developer Blues:} Maintained critical Salesforce sync alone for 3 years. No code reviews = no one to catch my mistakes. \\
\textbf{Data Horror:} Discovered 10 million records with "creative" JSON formats. My error handling code became larger than the actual logic. \\
\textbf{Golang Gamble:} Chose assignment specifically to learn Go professionally. Learning on production systems: not recommended. \\
\textbf{Lesson:} "It works on my machine" hits different when you're the only developer.

\subsection{Atlassian Add-on Business (2016 -- 2023)}
\textbf{Market Timing Fail:} Built successful Bamboo add-ons just as everyone migrated to cloud-based CI/CD. \\
\textbf{Support Nightmare:} 83 customers across 15 countries = 3am support calls in languages I don't speak. \\
\textbf{Lesson:} Building for dying platforms is profitable... until it isn't.
